🔥 Deflationary Mechanics
SEASON 1 · LIVE
Pack & Sink Burns
20% of every pack purchase and cosmetic spend is burned instantly on-chain at the moment of transaction.
SEASON 1 · LIVE
Buyback Burns
Treasury SOL from pump.fun fees periodically buys $9LV from the LP pool. 100% of purchased tokens burned. Announced publicly with tx hash.
SEASON 1 · EVERY 60 DAYS
Seasonal Treasury Burn
10% of treasury balance burned at season end. If the playerbase hits 1,000,000 collective points — an additional 1,000,000 $9LV burned on top.
SEASON 1 · EVERY 60 DAYS
Unclaimed Rewards Burn
Tokens in the rewards pool that go unclaimed at season end are burned — never rolled over. Claim your points before the season closes.
SEASON 2+
NFT Mint Burns
10% of all 2,500 Genesis NFT mint revenue is burned instantly at mint. The largest planned single deflationary event.
SEASON 2+
Boss Kill Burns
50,000 $9LV burned from treasury every time the community kills the weekly boss. Nerm announces each kill and burn.
