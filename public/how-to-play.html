🐱 What is Nine Lives Network?

Nine Lives Network is a social-competitive card game where you command a magical cat warrior called a Nine. Join a house, collect spell cards, and battle for territory across the realm of Nethara.

Play in 2–5 minute sessions throughout the day. Your Nine fights automatically every 15 minutes — you just deploy, play cards, and check results.

⚔️ Your Nine

When you register, you create your Nine with base stats determined by your house:

ATK
ATTACK POWER
HP
HEALTH POINTS
SPD
SPEED (WHO HITS FIRST)

Your Nine deploys to zones on the map. Equip spell cards to boost their stats and add powerful effects.

🃏 Spell Cards

Every card has ATK and HP stats that add to your Nine's power, plus special effects like BURN, HEAL, WARD, and more.

Charges: Cards have durability. Each use costs 1 charge. When charges run out, the card is exhausted — but never lost. Recharge by feeding duplicate or junk cards.

Open your daily pack (5 cards) to build your collection. Fuse 3 cards of the same rarity to craft the next tier up.

💧 Mana

Mana is your action currency. Every action costs 1 mana.

Regeneration: +1 mana every hour (up to 7 max).

Earn more: RT @NineLivesNetwork tweets for +1 bonus mana.

🎮 Game Modes

🔥
Zone Battles
Deploy your Nine to zones on the Nethara map. Every 15 minutes, combat auto-resolves — guild with most surviving HP controls the zone. Play cards to boost your Nine's power.
⚔️
Quick Duels
Challenge another player. Pick 3 cards each, reveal round by round. Higher ATK wins the round. Best of 3 takes it. Costs 1 mana.
🏰
The Gauntlet
Solo PvE. Fight through increasingly tough AI enemies, one card per floor. How far can you climb? Resets daily. Free to start.
🐉
Weekly Boss
A massive boss spawns every Monday. The whole server works together to take it down. Deploy cards to deal damage. Top contributors earn bonus rewards.

🏰 Guilds

Guilds are player teams (like crypto communities, DAOs, friend groups). Your guild fights together for zone control. Guild leaderboards track zones held, boss damage, and total power.

🏆 Seasons

Each season lasts ~8 weeks. Earn season points from zone control, duel wins, gauntlet depth, boss damage, and quest completions. Top players earn status rewards — design permanent spell cards, write zone names, appear in the lore.

💡 PRO TIP
Deploy your Nine to zones before bed. Combat cycles run 24/7 — Stonebark and Plaguemire builds dominate overnight while aggressive houses sleep.
