What is mana?
Mana is your daily energy. You get 5 mana per day. Each spell cast on 𝕏 or territory action on the map costs 1 mana. It resets at midnight UTC.
Can I change my house?
No — your house choice is permanent. Choose carefully! Each house has its own community and identity.
What is Nerm's Pick?
Each day, the AI bot Nerm selects 1-3 outstanding replies for a +25 point bonus. Write thoughtful, creative, or engaging replies to increase your chances.
How do streaks work?
Do any activity (reply, attack, defend) for consecutive days to build a streak. At 3 days you get ×1.1 on all points, 7 days gives ×1.2, and 14+ days gives ×1.3. Missing a day resets your streak to 0.
What is the War Room?
The War Room shows community competition. If you added a community tag (like $BONK), your points contribute to your community's total. Communities compete for rankings and bragging rights.
When does the daily story go live?
Every day at 8 AM UTC, @9LVNetwork posts a new story chapter. The story resolves at 11 PM UTC with results posted. Mana resets at midnight UTC.
What are lives?
Lives are used in the Arena (PvP duels). Losing a duel costs 1 life. Lives regenerate over time. The Arena is coming soon!
Is this free to play?
Yes! Nine Lives Network is completely free to play. You only need a 𝕏 account. NFTs and tokens are optional future features.
