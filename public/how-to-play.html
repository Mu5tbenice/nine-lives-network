How to Play

Become part of the story. Earn your place in Nethara.

1

Register here by connecting your 𝕏 (Twitter) account. This is how you'll cast spells and earn points.

Pick one of 9 magical houses. Each has its own identity, emblem, and community. This is permanent — choose wisely! Your house spells get bonus power.

Every day you get a free pack of 5 spell cards: 5 random spells from all 9 houses (including 4 universal). Each card has a rarity (Common → Legendary) that multiplies your power.

What is mana?
Mana is your daily energy for casting spells on the territory map. You get 7 mana per day. Each spell costs 1–3 mana depending on its tier. Mana is map-only — 𝕏 actions don't cost mana. It resets at midnight UTC.
What is Arcane Energy?
Arcane Energy (⚡) is earned by engaging on 𝕏 — replying, liking, retweeting, and quote tweeting the daily story. You need 30⚡ per day (plus spending all mana) to qualify for a card upgrade. It resets at midnight UTC.
How do card packs work?
Every day you get a free pack of 5 cards: 5 random spells drawn from all 9 houses plus 4 universal spells. Each card gets a random rarity (Common 55%, Uncommon 25%, Rare 12%, Epic 6%, Legendary 2%). Play them on the map to cast them.
How do card upgrades work?
If you spend all 7 mana AND earn 30⚡ in the same day, one card from your collection upgrades to the next rarity at midnight (Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary). You need BOTH — just playing on the site or just posting on 𝕏 isn't enough.
What is house affinity?
Everyone can use every spell, but casting your own house's spells gives +30% points and +20% influence. Allied house spells give +10% points. There's no penalty for using other houses' spells.
What do the effect pills do?
Effects like BURN, DRAIN, THORNS, HEAL modify what happens when you cast a card. BURN adds extra influence, DRAIN steals from the leading house, THORNS punishes attackers, HEAL restores influence. Hover over pills in the Spellbook to see descriptions.
Can I change my house?
No — your house choice is permanent. Choose carefully! Each house has its own community and identity.
What is Nerm's Pick?
Each day, the AI bot Nerm selects 1–3 outstanding replies for a +25 point bonus and +25⚡. Write thoughtful, creative, or engaging replies to increase your chances.
How do streaks work?
Do any activity (reply, cast, attack) for consecutive days to build a streak. At 3 days you get x1. on all points, 7 days gives x1., and 14+ days gives x1.. Missing a day resets your streak to 0.
Is this free to play?
Yes! Nine Lives Network is completely free to play. You only need a 𝕏 account. NFTs and tokens are optional future features.
When does the daily story go live?
Every day at 8 AM UTC, @9LVNetwork posts a new story chapter. The story resolves at 11 PM UTC with results posted. Mana resets at midnight UTC.
