📜 How to Play

Nine Lives Network is a social strategy game played on Twitter/X. Join a magical school, cast spells by tweeting, and fight for territory in the realm of Avaloris.

1

Register & Choose Your School

Sign in with your Twitter/X account and pledge allegiance to one of nine magical schools. Each school has its own identity and community.

You can also add a community tag (like $BONK or $WIF) to represent your crypto community on the leaderboards!

2

Watch for Daily Objectives

Every day, @9LVNetwork posts a daily objective announcing which territory is up for grabs.

🔔 Follow @9LVNetwork and turn on notifications so you never miss an objective!

The objective zone changes daily, and different zones have different bonuses.

3

Cast Spells by Replying

To cast a spell, simply reply to the daily objective tweet. That's it! Any reply counts as a spell cast.

Example replies:
"🔥 Ember bolt! The Covenant claims this territory!"

"Casting my vote for Radiant Order ✨"

"shadows consume all who oppose us 🌑"

Get creative! Roleplay your spell, trash talk rival schools, or just show up for your team. Every cast helps your school claim territory.

💡 You have 5 mana per day. Each cast costs 1 mana. Mana resets at midnight UTC.

4

Earn Points

Every spell cast earns you points. More creative and detailed casts earn bonus points!

Action Points
Any spell cast (Twitter) +10
Using school keywords +2
Descriptive cast (10-30 words) +3
Detailed cast (30+ words) +5
Casting in your home zone +20%
Nerm notices your spell +20
Spell of the Day award +50

Points contribute to three leaderboards: Individual, School, and Community.

5

Control Territory

When you cast a spell, you're fighting for territory on the map of Avaloris. The more your school casts, the more control you gain.

At the end of each day, the school with the most control over the objective zone wins! Check the Map to see current control percentages.

🏆 Winning schools earn bonus points and bragging rights!

FAQ

Can I change my school?
No, once you pledge to a school, you're locked in for the season. Choose wisely!
What if I run out of mana?
Wait until midnight UTC when your mana resets to 5. Plan your casts strategically!
Do I need to use specific spell words?
No! Any reply to the objective tweet counts. But using school-themed words earns bonus points.
What's a community tag?
Optional tag (like $BONK) that lets crypto communities compete on a separate leaderboard.
Is this free to play?
Yes! Just need a Twitter account. No wallet required for Season 0.
