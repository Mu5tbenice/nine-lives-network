📜 How to Play

Nine Lives Network is a social strategy game played on Twitter/X. Join a magical school, cast spells by tweeting, and fight for territory in Avaloris.

1

Register & Choose Your School

Sign in with Twitter and pledge allegiance to one of nine magical schools:

Add a community tag (like $BONK) to represent your crypto community!

2

Twitter Bounties (Free Points!)

Bounties are special tweets from @9LVNetwork that appear 2-3 times per day. Simply reply to earn points!

🎯 Bounties are FREE - no mana required. Reply once per bounty. Roleplay and ASCII art earn bonus points!

The school with the most participation wins the zone shown in the bounty tweet.

3

Territory Control (Costs Mana)

On the World page, you can attack or defend any territory. Each action costs 1 mana.

Attack = Try to flip a zone to your school's control
Defend = Protect zones your school controls

💧 You have 5 mana per day. Use wisely! Coordinate with your school to take key zones.

4

Duels (Costs Lives)

Challenge other players to 1v1 duels! Each duel costs 1 life (3 lives per day).

Win: +10 points | Lose: -10 points

Duels are currently 50/50 random. Future NFT holders will have stat-weighted odds!

FAQ

Can I change my school?
No, once you pledge, you're locked in for the season.
When do mana and lives reset?
Midnight UTC every day.
What's the Crystal Tower?
A visual showing faction power. The winning school at season end gets exclusive rewards!
Is this free to play?
Yes! Just need a Twitter account. NFTs are optional and coming later.
