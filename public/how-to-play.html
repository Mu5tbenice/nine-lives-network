Pick one of 9 magical houses. Each has its own identity, emblem, and community. This is permanent — choose wisely!
3
Rep Your Community
Rep your crypto community (like $BONK or $WIF) by entering your community tag. Your community is the primary competitive unit — communities compete for zone dominance on the territory map.
4
Engage with Game Posts
Throughout the day, @9LVNetwork posts story chapters, raids, and events. Reply, like, retweet, or quote tweet each post to earn points and Arcane Energy. Every post is a separate opportunity.

Open a free daily spell card pack containing 5 random cards. Cards are permanent collectibles in your collection — but each card is consumed when you cast it on a zone. Higher rarity cards deal more influence.

What is mana?
Mana is your daily energy for casting spells on the territory map. You get 7 mana per day. Each card cast on a zone costs 1 mana. Mana resets at midnight UTC.
Can I change my house?
No — your house choice is permanent. Choose carefully! Each house has its own community, identity, and spell cards.
What is Nerm's Pick?
Each day, the AI bot Nerm selects 1–3 outstanding replies for a +25 point bonus. Write thoughtful, creative, or entertaining replies to increase your chances.
How do streaks work?
Do any activity (reply, cast, attack) for consecutive days to build a streak. At 3 days you get ×1.1 on all points, 7 days gives ×1.2, and 14+ days gives ×1.3. Missing a day resets your streak to 0.
How does territory control work?
Every zone on the map is contestable. When you cast a card on a zone, your house's influence on that zone increases in real time. At midnight UTC, control is "banked" — whichever house has the most influence on each zone earns bonus points. After banking, influence decays by 30–50%, so defending a zone you already hold gives you an edge.
Can I earn points from multiple posts per day?
Yes! Each official @9LVNetwork post (stories, raids, events) is a separate opportunity. Reply, like, RT, or quote tweet each one. There's a daily soft cap of ~150–200 points from 𝕏 to keep things fair.
What happens when I cast a card?
Each card you cast on a zone is consumed (burned). You get 5 new cards every day from your daily pack. Build your collection strategically — save rare cards for important zones, use commons for daily coverage.
What are lives?
Lives are used in the Arena (PvP duels). Losing a duel costs 1 life. Lives reset to 3 at midnight UTC. Head to the Arena to duel rival Nines!
What are dominance charts?
The map shows real-time dominance charts for each zone — like crypto market cap charts but for house influence. Watch power shift throughout the day, track which houses are surging or declining, and time your attacks for maximum impact.
Is this free to play?
Yes! Nine Lives Network is completely free to play. You only need a 𝕏 account. NFTs and tokens are optional future features.
