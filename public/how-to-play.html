⚔️GAME MODES

🔥 Nethara Live — Territory Warfare

Deploy cards to zones. Combat cycles every 15 minutes. Control zones to earn points for your guild and house. The daily objective zone gives 1.5x bonus points.

⚔️ Quick Duels — PvP

Pick 3 cards. Best of 3 rounds against another player. Higher ATK wins each round. Costs 1 mana.

🏰 The Gauntlet — Solo PvE

Climb the tower floor by floor. Each floor has a tougher enemy. How high can you reach? Resets daily.

🐉 Boss Raid — Co-op

Every Monday a world boss spawns. All guilds unite to deal damage. Top damage dealers earn bonus rewards at the end of the week.

💧MANA SYSTEM

How Mana Works

You regenerate 1 mana per hour, up to 7 max. Most actions cost 1 mana. Earn bonus mana from daily quests and streaks. Every action you take matters — spend wisely!

🃏CARDS

Card Stats

Every card has ATK (attack power), HP (health), and a mana cost. Cards also have durability — they can only be used a set number of times before needing repair or replacement.

Rarities

🏰GUILDS & HOUSES

Guilds

Guilds are community teams (tied to your token ticker like $9LV). Guild members fight together for zone control and compete on guild leaderboards.

Houses

Houses are the 9 elemental factions of Nethara. Your house gives you identity and determines house standings. All zones are contestable — there are no home territories.
