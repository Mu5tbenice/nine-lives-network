Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

🐱 TL;DR: We collect your X profile info to run the game. We don't sell your data. We use cookies for basic functionality. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Introduction

Nine Lives Network ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

By using Nine Lives Network, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from X (Twitter)

When you sign in with X, we receive:

Data Why We Need It
X User ID Unique identifier for your account
Username (@handle) Display on leaderboards and in-game
Profile Image URL Display your avatar in the game
Account Creation Date Verify account requirements
Follower Count Verify account requirements

We do NOT have access to your X password, DMs, or ability to post on your behalf.

2.2 Information You Provide

  • School Selection — Which of the 9 schools you choose
  • Community Tag — Optional crypto community affiliation (e.g., $BONK)

2.3 Gameplay Data

  • Spell casts (replies to objective tweets)
  • Points earned
  • Territory contributions
  • Timestamps of activity

2.4 Automatically Collected

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • Pages visited and time spent

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your game account
  • Display your profile on leaderboards
  • Process spell casts and calculate points
  • Track territory control and game progress
  • Communicate game updates (via X, not email)
  • Detect and prevent cheating or abuse
  • Improve the game experience

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. Ever.

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers — Supabase (database), Replit (hosting), Anthropic (AI)
  • Other Players — Your username, school, points, and rank are publicly visible on leaderboards
  • Legal Requirements — If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to all players:

  • Your X username and profile image
  • Your school affiliation
  • Your community tag (if provided)
  • Your points and leaderboard rank
  • Your spell casts (these are public X replies anyway)

If you don't want this information public, please don't play. The game is inherently social.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on:

  • Supabase — PostgreSQL database with encryption at rest
  • Replit — Application hosting with HTTPS

We implement reasonable security measures, but no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

  • Local Storage — To remember your player ID for login persistence
  • Session Cookies — For authentication state during OAuth

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics (yet). If we add them, we'll update this policy.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your data
  • Correction — Ask us to fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us via X at @Mustbenice.

9. Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. DM @Mustbenice on X
  2. Request account deletion
  3. We'll verify your identity and delete your data within 30 days

Note: Public leaderboard history may be retained in anonymized form.

10. Children's Privacy

Nine Lives Network is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. International Users

Our servers are located in the United States. If you're accessing the Game from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the US.

For EU/UK users: By using the Game, you consent to this transfer. We aim to comply with GDPR principles.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll post the new policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes means you accept the new policy.

13. Third-Party Links

The Game may contain links to third-party websites (like X). We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.
